# Case: Product Reliability Roadmap

## Prompt

Turn LegalCode into a product that solo litigators can rely on. Start with US litigation, then India, then other jurisdictions. Specify the matter command center, role-specific legal coworkers, trust layer, litigation workflows, legal documents, structured legal sheets, collaboration model, local-first storage, cloud sync, jurisdiction packs, tests, and acceptance criteria.

## Expected Qualities

- Prioritizes US solo litigators and human-in-the-loop supervised automation.
- Defines matter workspaces with parties, claims, deadlines, documents, evidence, research, pleadings, notes, tasks, and audit log.
- Uses role-specific agents for strategy, research, drafting, discovery, citations, deadlines, evidence chronology, settlement, filing, and workspace integration.
- Requires source spans, confidence, unresolved questions, citation validation, quote verification, fake-citation detection, and verify-before-filing status.
- Makes ProseMirror/Yjs legal documents and Yjs-backed structured legal sheets the first collaboration engines.
- Keeps V1 sheets as typed legal work tables rather than full spreadsheet formula engines.
- Preserves local-first encrypted matter storage and enables cloud sync only for invited matter collaborators.
- Requires agent action provenance and denies unauthorized context access outside the selected matter.
- Includes golden matter fixtures, trust evals, workflow tests, collaboration tests, security tests, and explicit acceptance criteria.
