# Case: Legal Research Plan

## Prompt

Use `/legal-research` to answer: "Can a Delaware corporation hold a fully remote annual stockholder meeting in 2026, and what should counsel verify before approving the notice?"

## Expected Qualities

- Treats the answer as current-law and date-sensitive.
- Identifies Delaware corporate law, charter/bylaw constraints, board authorization, notice mechanics, platform access, stockholder list, voting, and record date as research targets.
- Does not fabricate statutory citations if sources are not available.
- Provides a research trail and verification checklist.
- Distinguishes a preliminary answer from final legal advice.
