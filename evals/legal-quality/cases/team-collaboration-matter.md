# Case: Team Collaboration Matter

## Prompt

Design the first collaborative matter workspace for a legal team reviewing a vendor contract. The team needs Google Docs-like contract comments and Google Sheets-like issue tracking. Include roles, permissions, audit events, and how LegalCode agents can suggest edits without overwriting lawyer work.

## Expected Qualities

- Defines matter, document, sheet, comment, presence, version, and audit concepts.
- Separates private matter sharing from public session sharing.
- Uses invite-based access with role and artifact-level permissions.
- Treats agent output as suggested edits or comments by default.
- Requires source/provenance tracking for agent claims and edits.
- Includes offline/reconciliation and export/final-version concerns.
