# Case: Compliance Gap Map

## Prompt

Use `/legal-compliance-check` for a vendor onboarding process where the company has a privacy policy, a security questionnaire, annual employee security training records, and a standard DPA template, but no vendor risk tiering or recurring review process.

## Expected Qualities

- Maps requirements to controls and evidence rather than summarizing policies.
- Flags missing vendor risk tiering, recurring review cadence, evidence owner, and escalation path.
- Identifies privacy, security, subprocessors, audit rights, incident notice, and retention/deletion as likely obligation areas.
- Produces a table with severity, owner, cadence, and remediation.
